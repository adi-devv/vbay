VBay

The VBay app by VBay Co. is a campus-focused peer-to-peer marketplace designed to facilitate buying, selling, and trading among college students. It offers a platform for students to list items such as textbooks, electronics, bicycles, and other goods, as well as to post "seeks" for items they are seeking.
